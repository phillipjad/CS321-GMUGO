About Us

This app was created by a team consisting of seven people.

We're students at GMU and, knowing the struggle of trying to find things to do in the area, we built this app to help others like us.

What We Offer

We offer tools to help you find events, attractions, and more around the Northern Virginia area!

With this web application, you will be find yourself with plenty to do!

To get started, navigate to the "My Interests" section, enter some details about yourself, and then navigate back to the "Itinerary" tab to see the magic of our service!
